# #42 Invisible Char Remover

A hyper-minimalist "digital vacuum" to sweep away hidden, problematic characters from your text.

### 1 Tool / 1 Action
- **The Problem:** Hidden full-width spaces, zero-width spaces, and control characters often cause errors in coding, formatting, or data entry.
- **The Solution:** Paste your text and tap "ERASE IT" to instantly sanitize your data.

### Features
- **Smart Detection:** Identifies and counts specific "invisible" culprits.
- **Auto-Cleaning:** Removes:
  - Full-width Spaces (U+3000)
  - Zero-width Spaces (U+200B)
  - Tab Characters (U+0009)
  - Control Characters (hidden system codes)
- **One-Tap Copy:** Get your clean text back to the clipboard instantly.
- **Privacy First:** Browser-only processing. Your text never leaves your device.

### Design System
- **Fridge Combo Standard:** White background, #333 black buttons, 15px rounded corners.
- **Mobile Optimized:** Large input area and thumb-friendly "CLR" button placement.

### How to Use
1. Paste your "dirty" text into the box.
2. Tap **ERASE IT**.
3. Check the "Cleaned!" report to see what was found.
4. Tap **COPY CLEANED TEXT**.
